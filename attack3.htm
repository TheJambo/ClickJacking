What is Likejacking?

Likejacking, a form of clickjacking, is a malicious technique of tricking users of a website into posting a Facebook status update for a site they did not intentionally mean to "like".[1] The initial concept and code for likejacking were created by a Black Hat World user that goes by the handle izSpK.[citation needed]

The term "likejacking" came from a comment posted by Corey Ballou[2] in the article How to "Like" Anything on the Web (Safely), which is one of the first documented postings explaining the possibility of malicious activity regarding Facebook's "like" button.[3]
